Naija Recipes Gallery - Site Plan

Site Name

Naija Recipes Gallery – This name reflects a colorful and cultural collection of Nigerian recipes, both old and new. It captures the vibe of traditional and modern flavors in one single site.

Optional domain availability: naijarecipesgallery.netlify.app or via GitHub Pages

Site Purpose

This site go serve as a gallery of popular Nigerian recipes. E go showcase both traditional meals like Egusi, Oha, and Moi Moi, as well as modern twists like Shawarma Suya or Indomie Jollof. The goal na to promote Nigerian food culture and make people sabi how to prepare each recipe. Users go fit view recipe images, read short descriptions, and navigate recipes by category.

Scenarios

  • How do I cook native jollof rice wey get that party smoke flavor?
  • Which Naija meals dey simple for breakfast or lunch box ideas?
  • Wetin be the difference between northern and southern suya?

Color Schema

  • #a52a2a – Deep Brown: For headings and buttons (symbolizing food and spice)
  • #fffaf5 – Soft Off-White: For background (to make content pop)
  • #f4a460 – Light Orange: For accents (highlight, hover effects)

Typography

  • Headings: 'Segoe UI', sans-serif
  • Body Text: 'Verdana', sans-serif
  • Special Highlights: 'Courier New' for code-like effects or small titles
